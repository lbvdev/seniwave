What is SENI WAVE?

The name comes from “seni” (“contemporary art” in Indonesian) and “wave,” the energy of digital innovation. Our white and purple palette reflects our belief in clarity, optimism, and the real value technology brings.

Real Story?

SENI WAVE: Created for founders, by someone who’s been there.

But the real story starts with our founder’s own experience: working as a product manager, navigating the maze of branding, web development, and setup—often piecing things together with freelancers.
We saw how first-time founders and business owners end up overwhelmed—forced to learn new skills, overpay for partial solutions, or settle for websites that can’t really grow with them. We believed there was a better way.

How We Work

SENIWAVE brings together talented creators—designers, AI experts, developers, 3D and AR specialists with a base in Dubai and a network across Europe and Russia. Every project is personally led by our founder, ensuring each case gets the care and attention.
We guide founders from raw idea to launch-ready product, without lock-in or unnecessary costs.

Our Approach

Quality over quantity: we take on a limited number of projects to maintain our high standards and deliver real results.
